computer

computer is programmable electronic device that accept data (input), process the data , can store the data or results , provide the user with information (output)

Data VS. Information

  • Data

    is a representation of a fact ,figure or idea. data can be a number ,a word ,a picture ,or even a recording of sound

  • Information

    is a data that has been organized or presented in a meaningful fashion

    The language of computer:

    computer uses combination of hardware and software to process data into information

  • hardware

    is any part of the computer that you can physically touch

  • software

    is a set of computer programs

        Types of computer:

        A-Stationary

      1. Desktop computer : can be used at work, home, and school. Desktop computers are designed to be placed on a desk, and they're typically made up of a few different parts, including the computer case, monitor, keyboard, and mouse.
      2. Embedded computer:Self-contained computer devices performing dedicated functions- Specially designed computer chip that resides in another device.
      3. Supercomputer:is a computer that performs at or near the currently highest operational rate for computers. Traditionally, supercomputers have been used for scientific and engineering applications that must handle very large databases or do a great amount of computation (or both).

        B- Portable

      4. Laptop computer : often called a notebook, is a small, portable personal computer (PC) with a "clamshell" form factor, typically having a thin LCD or LED computer screen mounted on the inside of the upper lid of the clamshell and an alphanumeric keyboard on the inside of the lower lid.
      5. Tablet computer: commonly shortened to tablet, is a mobile device, typically with a mobile operating system and touchscreen display processing circuitry, and a rechargeable battery in a single, thin and flat package.
